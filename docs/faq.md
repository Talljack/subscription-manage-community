## English Version

### About WhereMyMoney

#### What is WhereMyMoney?

WhereMyMoney is a smart subscription management app that helps users track, manage, and optimize subscription expenses.

#### Is WhereMyMoney free?

Yes! We offer three plans:
- **Free**: Up to 5 subscriptions
- **Basic**: $2.99/month, up to 50 subscriptions
- **Pro**: $5.99/month, unlimited subscriptions + advanced features

#### How do I get started?

1. Visit [wheremymoney.pro](https://wheremymoney.pro)
2. Sign in with Google or GitHub
3. Add your first subscription
4. Set reminders and budgets

#### How do I ask a question?

3. Provide relevant info (subscription type, environment)

### Subscription Management

#### How do I cancel a subscription?

2. Check the provider's help docs

Use WhereMyMoney app:
- Set renewal reminders
- Get notified 7 days early
- View subscription calendar

#### I was charged twice, what do I do?

1. Check billing cycle (monthly/yearly)
2. Contact customer support
3. Share your experience in the community
